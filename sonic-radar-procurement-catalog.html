Long Description

The SR-9000 Industrial Sonic Radar is Wavetek Sensing Solutions GmbH's most advanced long-range acoustic detection platform, designed to operate in extreme industrial and environmental conditions. Unlike conventional ultrasonic proximity sensors, the SR-9000 uses phased-array acoustic beam steering to achieve detection ranges of up to 280 metres in open-air deployments and 75 metres through dense solid media such as rock, soil, and reinforced concrete.

The unit's ruggedised enclosure is rated IP67 for full dust exclusion and temporary water immersion, and its ATEX Zone 2 certification permits deployment in environments with explosive gas atmospheres — making it suitable for use in oil refineries, chemical processing plants, and mining operations. An integrated vibration isolation mount minimises interference from machinery-induced ground vibration.

SR-9000 的核心传感模块采用压电复合晶体阵列技术，支持 20 Hz–80 kHz 超宽声学频段扫描。内置 DSP 数字信号处理单元可实时滤除环境噪声，目标定位精度达 ±0.05°。设备配备以太网、RS-485 及 CAN 总线接口，可无缝集成至工厂自动化平台及 SCADA 监控系统。
